How to be a vegetarian

One of the question I get asked the most is "how have you been a vegetarian for so long?!" so I'm here to tell you all how to make it really easy for yourself to be a vegetarian!

First steps to be successful in your veggie journey

  • Determination
  • Starting small
  • Learn to substitute
  • Try new recipes
  • Think about protein
  • Forget about Junk Food
Reminder!

This transition is not easy - it should be hard for you to give up something you're used to eating. If you're a picky eater who only eats burgers and ham sandwiches it will take you longer to get used to your new diets so START SMALL.

Another ways to start your process ...

  1. Make a Vegetarian version of one of your favorite meals
  2. Explore recipes online - you can find meat replacements in your grocery store
  3. Try some of the meat-free and dairy-free products
  4. Search for any Mexican, Indian, Ethiopian restaurants near you , they have a lot of vegetarian options in their menus!
  5. If you don't think you can do this forever maybe you should just become a Flexitarian
